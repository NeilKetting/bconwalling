About Us

Bcon Walling is a premier provider of fencing and walling solutions, established with a commitment to quality and client satisfaction. With 17 years of experience in the construction industry, we have honed our expertise in high-level security walling, precast walling, fencing, and aluminum installations. Our journey began with a vision to deliver robust and reliable solutions that stand the test of time.

Over the years, Bcon Walling has successfully completed a wide range of projects, from residential installations to large-scale commercial endeavors. Our extensive knowledge of construction techniques, combined with our dedication to using top-quality materials, allows us to execute every project with precision and care. We utilize massive concrete prefabricated panels, ensuring that our installations meet the highest standards of durability and security.

At Bcon Walling, we pride ourselves on our client-centric approach. We believe that strong relationships with our clients are the foundation of our success. Our team works closely with you to understand your specific needs, providing tailored solutions that exceed your expectations. We handle every aspect of the project, including excavation, compaction, and installation, all while adhering to engineer specifications to guarantee optimal results.
